Breakfast Egg Rolls

Home

Description

A fun take on a breakfast classic. Traditional egg roll wrappers are used to hold scrambled eggs, sausage, bell peppers and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • Ground sausage (I prefer turkey sausage)
  • Egg roll wrappers
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the sausage in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Remove and drain the sausage.
  3. Add the bell peppers to the pan and sautee for ~5 minutes.
  4. Return the drained sausage to the pan.
  5. Add the eggs to the pan.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. When the mixture is beginning to bind, add shredded cheese.
  8. Remove from heat when mixture is sufficiently binded.
  9. Preheat oven to 400 degF.
  10. Fill each egg roll wrapper with a spoonful of the mixture. Use cold water to close the wrapper.
  11. Place filled wrappers on a greased sheet pan.
  12. Once all wrappers are on the pan, spray each with a small amount of cooking spray.
  13. Cook for 22 minutes, flipping halfway.
  14. Remove and enjoy!
